Hampton Court Palace

Hampton Court Palace Ice Rink is situated in one of the most scenic settings in London! Skate with the magnificent palace as the backdrop to a magical night.

  • The Ice Rink is open from 25 November to 8 January
  • 11am until 10pm every day except Christmas Day
  • Prices start from 8.55GBP
  • Address: Molesey, East Molesey KT8 9AU, UK


Natural History Museum

The Natural History Museum offers an amazing dackdrop filled with fairy lights and frost-covered trees. This is the perfect way to get ready for the festive season with the smell of mulled wine and toasted marshmallow filling the air.

  • The Ice Rink is open from 27 October to 8 January
  • 11am until 10pm every day
  • Prices start from 8.80GBP
  • Address: Cromwell Rd, Kensington, London SW7 5BD, UK


Canary Wharf

The Luminocity Ice Rink Canary Wharf is brightening up Canada Square Park this winter with more than 8km of LED lights laid beneath the ice. LUMINOCITY will showcase more than 16 million different colours every session and swathe skaters in an abundance of magical light to create a truly immersive skating experience.

  • The Ice Rink is open from 4 November to 4 February
  • 10am until 10pm
  • Prices start from 9.95GBP
  • Address: Canada Square Park, Canary Wharf, London E14 5AB, UK


Tower of London

The Tower of London ice rink returns this winter, offering day and evening ice skating sessions in the setting of the historic London landmark. Catch up with friends and family on the ice or enjoy a romantic skate under the starry sky, as hundreds of snowflakes light up the wall of the fortress after dark.

  • The Ice Rink is open from 6 November to 3 January
  • 11am until 10pm
  • Prices: Adult ticket 13.50GBP and Child ticket 9.50GBP
  • Address: St Katharine's and Wapping, London EC3N 4AB, UK
